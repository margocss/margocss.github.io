Text Formats

Default font is Libre Franklin from google fonts. The text right here is inside of a <p> tag. All text sizes and line heights are calculated based on ems. The standard paragraph text is sized at 1em. And the default line-height is set to 1.25em on mobile to accomodate the lesser screen height and 1.625em on tablet/desktop for ideal legibility.
