La

Condesa

Chef Edguardo Arreizga

In the heart of Bogota in an intimate bistro setting, chef Edguardo Areizaga offers the best of traditional American comfort food prepared with haute cuisine techniques. The ingredients are obtained from top quality local sources, featuring a high percentage of organic produce and meats. The menu changes weekly, offering a mix of new and signature dishes.
